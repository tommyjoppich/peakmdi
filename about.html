Our History

Founded in 2007, PEAK MEDICAL Distribution Inc. (PEAK) is an independently owned medical device company focused on delivering high-quality surgical instruments through a personalized, service-driven approach. Backed by decades of industry experience, PEAK provides reliable products, responsive support, and consistent value to healthcare providers.

Established by Bob Cvengros, PEAK began with a focus on stainless steel and titanium hand-held instruments. As the company grew, it obtained FDA registration and expanded into contract manufacturing of Class I devices. Today, PEAK continues to evolve—broadening its product offering and advancing into innovative solutions that meet the demands of a rapidly changing surgical landscape.

Our portfolio spans Cardiac, Vascular, Neuro, Micro, Orthopaedic, and General Surgery. In addition to standard instrumentation, PEAK specializes in sourcing and manufacturing legacy and discontinued products that remain critical to hospital systems.

Through partnerships with leading global manufacturers and U.S.-based machine operations, PEAK combines advanced technology with skilled craftsmanship. We also place a strong emphasis on medical education—ensuring commonly used instruments are properly understood and utilized to maximize performance and longevity.
